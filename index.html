FAQ

Questions before login.

How do members get access?

They log in with Discord. The portal checks that they are in your server and have the paid role.

What happens when a payment ends?

Stripe webhooks should update the user record and tell the Discord bot to remove the paid role.

Can this store card data?

No raw card numbers or CVVs should be stored. Stripe should handle payment data.
